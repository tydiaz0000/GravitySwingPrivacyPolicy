Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 27, 2026

Thank you for playing Gravity Swing. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

Information We Collect

1. Username
The username you choose for leaderboards and game features.

2. Device Identifier
A unique device or app-generated identifier to recognize returning users, save progress, and support leaderboard functionality.

3. Device Information

  • Device model
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • Processor type
  • Memory size
  • Graphics device information
  • Screen resolution

4. App Interactions

  • App launches
  • Game starts and completed runs
  • Scores and leaderboard activity
  • Feature usage
  • Error reports and diagnostics

How We Use Information

  • Operate game services and leaderboards
  • Improve performance and compatibility
  • Detect abuse or cheating
  • Analyze gameplay trends
  • Respond to feedback requests

Hosting and Data Processing

All backend systems and stored data are hosted directly by the developer on privately managed servers. No third-party analytics or outsourced backend processors are used for core operations.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Data may only be disclosed if required by law or necessary to protect system integrity.

Security

Reasonable safeguards are used to protect collected data. However, no internet transmission or storage system is completely secure.

Your Choices

You may stop data collection by uninstalling the game. Some features such as leaderboards require identifiers to function.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted in the app or store listing.

Contact

For privacy inquiries, please contact the developer through the official store listing.
